Introduction

Sloths are slow-moving mammals found in Central and South America. They are known for their slow metabolism and their relaxed lifestyle, spending most of their time in trees.

Types of Sloths

There are two main types of sloths: two-toed and three-toed sloths. While they share many similarities, they belong to different families and have distinct characteristics.

Habitat and Diet

Sloths primarily live in rainforests, where they can easily blend into the environment. Their diet consists mostly of leaves, which are low in nutrients and contribute to their slow energy metabolism.

Behavior

Sloths are known for their slow and deliberate movements. They sleep for up to 20 hours a day and have adapted to spending most of their lives hanging upside down in trees.
